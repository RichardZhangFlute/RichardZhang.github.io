  • Learn More About Me
Hailing from Cupertino, California, Richard will study under the tutelage of Professor Thomas
Robertello at Indiana University’s Jacobs School of Music. He started studying with Dr. Ming Li,
and now currently studies with Dr. Ray Furuta. He is a part of the Wind Ensemble One, Jazz
Ensemble, and Symphony Orchestra at Homestead High School, and is Principal Flute at the
California Philharmonic Youth Orchestra. For the 2021-22 orchestral season, as a winner of the
annual concerto competition, Richard performed Mozart’s Flute Concerto in G Major. Richard
has also participated in the Santa Clara County Honor Band in 2021 and 2022 as the second
chair as flute one as well as piccolo. He has also been awarded a Command Superior Award at
the CMEA Bay Section Solo and Ensemble Festival. In the summer of 2021, Richard attended
Juilliard Summer Winds and Beyond the Masterclass. Richard has performed at masterclasses
for Carol Wincenc, Robert Langevin, Jim Walker, Jill Felber, and Catherine Karoly.
